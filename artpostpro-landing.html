What it does

Everything your gallery needs to
show up consistently.

Built specifically for fine art galleries. Not a generic social media tool retrofitted for art — a purpose-built platform that understands how galleries work.

Artwork Image Branding

Upload any artwork image and instantly generate a branded 1080×1080px post with your gallery logo, colors, and artwork details. Download or schedule directly.

Smart Post Scheduling

Build a week or month of content in one sitting. Schedule posts to Instagram, Facebook, and more at optimal times without lifting a finger on posting day.

AI Caption Generator

Enter the artwork title, artist, and medium — get a polished, gallery-appropriate caption in seconds. Customize the tone to match your brand voice.

Multi-Platform Publishing

One post, every platform. Publish simultaneously to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Pinterest — each formatted correctly for the platform.

Performance Analytics

See which artworks drive the most engagement, which captions perform best, and when your audience is most active. Make data-driven decisions about what to post next.

Gallery Content Library

Store every artwork image, branded post, and caption in one place. Build a reusable content library that grows with your collection and never loses an asset.
